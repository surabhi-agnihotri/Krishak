About Krishak, Inc.

At Krishak, our mission is to connect farmers with the farm equipment they need, affordably and efficiently. Through our web-based platform, we link farmers seeking specific machinery with owners willing to share or rent. This reduces financial strain on small and medium-sized farmers, providing access to equipment at a fraction of the cost of ownership. Additionally, our intelligent scheduling and resource allocation algorithms ensure optimal equipment utilization, minimizing downtime and maximizing productivity.
